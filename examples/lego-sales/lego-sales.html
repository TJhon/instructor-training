Grouping by age

Try using mutate() and case_when()!
Do you need some help with logical operators? Remember

"18 and under" : age <= 18
     "19 - 25" : age >= 19 & age <= 25

etc.
lego_sales <- lego_sales %>%
  mutate(age_group = case_when(
    age <= 18             ~ "18 and under",
    ___
  ))
lego_sales <- lego_sales %>%
  mutate(age_group = case_when(
    age <= 18 ~ "18 and under",
    age >= 19 & age <= 25 ~ "19 - 25",
    age >= 26 & age <= 35 ~ "26 - 35",
    age >= 36 & age <= 50 ~ "36 - 50",
    age >= 51 ~ "51 and over"
  ))
grade_result(
